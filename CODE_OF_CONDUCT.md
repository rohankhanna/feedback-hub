# Code Of Conduct

## Expected Conduct
People participating in this repository should:
- be respectful and direct
- focus criticism on ideas, code, and behavior rather than people
- keep technical disagreement concrete and evidence-based
- avoid harassment, abuse, and personal attacks

## Unacceptable Conduct
- harassment or intimidation
- hateful or discriminatory language
- doxxing or threats
- sustained bad-faith disruption
- publishing private information without permission

## Scope
This applies to issues, pull requests, reviews, and other public project interactions tied to this repository.

## Enforcement
The maintainer may remove content, close threads, or block participation when conduct harms the project or other participants.
